You are a Senior Business Analyst and Product Owner working in an enterprise SaaS organization.

Create a detailed, implementation-ready Business Requirements Document (BRD) for the following system:

System Name: AI-Assisted Customer Support Ticketing System

The BRD must be written in a professional, enterprise tone and be suitable for:
- Stakeholder review and approval
- Backend and frontend engineering teams
- Architecture and design discussions

The document must include the following sections and level of detail:

1. Introduction & Purpose
   - Overview of the problem the system solves
   - Purpose of the system
   - Target users and audience

2. Business Goals & Objectives
   - Operational efficiency goals
   - Customer satisfaction goals
   - Scalability and data integrity goals

3. Scope of the System
   - In-scope features
   - Out-of-scope items (explicitly listed)

4. Stakeholders & User Personas
   - Customer
   - Support Agent
   - Support Manager
   - Admin
   - Describe responsibilities clearly in table format

5. Key Features & Functional Requirements
   - Authentication & Authorization (JWT, RBAC)
   - Ticket lifecycle management
   - Comments and audit history
   - SLA tracking and escalation
   - Dashboards per role
   - Use numbered functional requirements (FR-01, FR-02, etc.)

6. AI-Assisted Capabilities
   - Automatic ticket categorization
   - Priority prediction
   - AI-generated response suggestions
   - Optional/future AI features clearly marked

7. Non-Functional Requirements
   - Performance targets
   - Security requirements
   - Availability and resilience
   - UI/UX expectations

8. Constraints & Assumptions
   - Technology stack assumptions
   - Language and deployment constraints

9. Success Metrics
   - System uptime
   - AI accuracy
   - User success indicators

Formatting Guidelines:
- Use clear headings and sub-headings
- Use bullet points and tables where appropriate
- Keep language clear, concise, and unambiguous
- Ensure requirements are realistic and implementation-ready
- Maintain enterprise documentation standards

Output:
A complete, well-structured Business Requirements Document (BRD) in Markdown format.
